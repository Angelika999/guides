## Convert Decimals to Fractions

Using d = decimal.

Divide d by 1 so you have d/1 then multiply top and bottom by 100, creating 100d/100.
Example: convert 0.2 into a fraction:
0.2/1 ---> x100 = 20/100 = 2/10.
